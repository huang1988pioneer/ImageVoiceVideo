# Product

## Register

product

## Platform

web

## Users

Content creators who need a narrated clip from a still image—often for social posts, explainers, or multi-language variants. They arrive with a picture and a script in mind, work in short sessions, and want a finished file without opening a full video editor.

## Product Purpose

**Image Voice Video** turns one image plus a script into a downloadable video with AI voiceover and multi-language subtitles. Success is leaving with a usable MP4 or WebM in minutes, not configuring a production suite.

## Positioning

Image + script becomes a multi-language narrated video in one continuous flow—no separate editor, no timeline.

## Brand Personality

**Fast, clear, capable.** Voice is direct and tool-like: short status lines, obvious next steps, confidence without hype. Emotionally the product should feel like a sharp utility that respects the creator’s time.

## Anti-references

Generic SaaS dashboards—sidebar navigation, metric cards, enterprise gray chrome. This is a focused creator workspace, not an admin console.

## Design Principles

1. **One continuous flow** — Upload, script, language tracks, settings, preview, and generate live in a single workspace; never fragment the job across app sections for their own sake.
2. **Preview is truth** — What the preview shows (framing, subtitles, orientation) is what the export delivers.
3. **Minutes to download** — Every control earns its place by speeding a usable clip, not by showcasing power.
4. **Clarity over chrome** — Prefer readable hierarchy and obvious primary actions over decorative density.
5. **Languages are first-class** — Multi-track voice and captions are core to the product, not an afterthought buried in settings.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Solid practical defaults: visible keyboard focus, sufficient contrast for body and UI text, and respect for `prefers-reduced-motion`. Aim for WCAG AA where practical; formal certification is not a hard gate yet.
